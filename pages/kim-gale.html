Kim Gale

Or, The Gumpy Archives.

Spoiler alert: This is likely of zero interest to anyone I am not related to.

So it turns out Gramps was more active on the internet that most people my age's grandpas. Aside from writing his "Kim Gale's Fishtales" article for the Emeryville Sportfishing center, he posted fishing reports here and there, was a member of BMUG (Berkeley Mac User's Group), and was not afraid to email webmasters (which I'm told were a thing back then). Since most of this was posted in the 1990s the only traces of it are dead links in cached Google search results. Bits and pieces of his work can be found in Archive.org's Wayback Machine though.

I've scraped just about all I could find off of that site. As I clean it up, I'll post it here for friends and family.
